FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
GET TO KNOW US BETTER

How long does it take to get my first data delivery?

Most projects deliver data within 48 hours of our discovery call. Simple projects arrive in 24 hours, while complex multi-source engagements take three to five days.

What if the website blocks your scrapers?

We use rotating proxies, CAPTCHA solving, and browser automation to work around anti-bot systems. Our success rate is above 95%, and we alert you if alternatives are required before billing.

How much does MRI Data cost?

Pricing is project-based and depends on complexity, data volume, and frequency. Most projects range from $500 to $3,000 per month. You receive a custom quote after the discovery call.

Do you offer a free trial or sample data?

We deliver a 50-100 record sample dataset during scoping at no cost. This lets you validate quality and format before committing to the full project.

What happens if the website structure changes?

We monitor scrapers daily and update them when structures change. Fixes usually arrive within hours, and maintenance is included in your monthly cost.

Is web scraping legal?

We scrape publicly accessible data and follow legal best practices. We respect privacy regulations and only work on compliant use cases.

Can you integrate with our existing systems?

Yes. We deliver to databases, APIs, cloud storage, or CRMs. Every project includes tailored delivery that fits your workflow.

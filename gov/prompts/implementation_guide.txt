## FINAL NOTES FOR IMPLEMENTATION

### Design Philosophy
This landing page follows institutional, governmental aesthetic (whitehouse.gov inspired) to convey trust, authority, and reliability. The design is intentionally restrainedâ€”no gradients, no playful illustrations, no trendy design elements. Every choice reinforces credibility and professionalism.

- **Data-Driven**: Metrics and statistics prominent
- **Enterprise-Ready**: Security, compliance, integration mentions
- **Scalability Implied**: "From 10 to 10,000" messaging

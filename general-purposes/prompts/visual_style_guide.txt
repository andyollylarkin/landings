## üéØ 18. CONVERSION OPTIMIZATION PRINCIPLES

### CTA Placement
```
Above the fold: Primary CTA in hero section
Sticky CTA: Mobile bottom bar (48px height)
Section CTAs: End of each major section
Exit Intent: Modal with special offer
Floating Button: Bottom-right corner (desktop)
```

### Visual Hierarchy Rules
```
1. Hero headline: Largest, boldest, highest contrast
2. Sub-headline: 60% size of headline
3. Body text: Clear contrast, comfortable reading size
4. CTAs: High contrast, prominent placement
5. Supporting info: Subdued but readable
```

## 10. TECHNICAL IMPLEMENTATION NOTES

### Performance Optimization
- **Target Load Time:** <2 seconds
- **Image Format:** WebP with JPG fallback
- **Image Compression:** Maximum 200KB per image
- **Video:** Compressed MP4, max 5MB
- **Lazy Loading:** All images below fold
- **Critical CSS:** Inline above-fold styles
- **Font Loading:** System fonts with web font enhancement
- **CDN:** All static assets on CDN

### Responsive Breakpoints
- **Mobile:** 320px - 767px
- **Tablet:** 768px - 1023px
- **Desktop:** 1024px - 1440px
- **Large Desktop:** 1441px+

### Mobile-Specific Adjustments
- **Font sizes:** Reduce by 20-30%
- **Padding:** Reduce by 40-50%
- **CTA buttons:** Full-width (minus 24px margins)
- **Cards:** Stack vertically
- **Tables:** Horizontal scroll or transform to cards
- **Video:** Thumbnail only, click to play (save bandwidth)

### Analytics & Tracking

#### Conversion Goals to Track:
1. Demo scheduled (primary goal)
2. Email captured (lead magnet)
3. Video played (engagement)
4. Scroll depth (50%, 75%, 100%)
5. CTA clicks (by position)
6. FAQ expansions (by question)
7. Time on page
8. Exit rate by section

#### Heatmap Focus Areas:
- Hero CTA engagement
- Problem section resonance
- FAQ interaction patterns
- Exit points

### A/B Test Priority List (Post-Launch)

**Week 1-2:**
1. Headline variations (outcome-driven vs. problem-solution)
2. CTA button text ("Schedule Demo" vs. "Get Free Sample")

**Week 3-4:**
3. Hero visual (video vs. static dashboard)
4. Social proof position (above vs. below benefits)

6. Color scheme (orange vs. blue CTAs)

**Month 2:**
7. Testimonial formats (text vs. video)

## FINAL NOTES FOR IMPLEMENTATION

### Content Tone & Voice:
- **Professional but approachable:** Use "you/your" language
- **Confident without arrogance:** "We deliver" not "We're the best"
- **Specific over generic:** Numbers, timelines, concrete examples
- **Active voice:** "We extract data" not "Data is extracted"
- **Concise:** Every word earns its place

### Visual Hierarchy Principles:
1. **Most important:** Hero headline + CTA
2. **Secondary:** Problem section, benefits
3. **Supporting:** Social proof, FAQs
4. **Contextual:** Footer, legal

### Color Psychology:
- **Orange (primary):** Energy, action, innovation
- **Navy (secondary):** Trust, stability, professionalism
- **White (background):** Cleanliness, simplicity
- **Gray (text):** Readability, sophistication
- **Green (accents):** Success, growth, positive outcomes

### Typography System:
- **Headlines:** Inter/Poppins (bold, modern)
- **Body:** Open Sans/System font (readable)
- **Accents:** Monospace for code/data references

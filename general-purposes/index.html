  • THE CLIENT: A global beverage company launching a new energy drink.

    THE CHALLENGE: Pre-launch marketing sentiment was unclear. Were their ads resonating? Was the branding hitting the right note with their target audience?

    THE MRI SCAN: We deployed a real-time sentiment analysis scan across Instagram, X (Twitter), and public Facebook groups. We tracked brand mentions, competitor mentions, and key campaign hashtags, analyzing thousands of posts and comments.

    THE HIDDEN TRUTH: The data revealed a significant disconnect. While users on Instagram loved the can design, a vocal community on X was criticizing the brand name as "trying too hard to be cool," creating a potential reputational risk.

    THE RESULT: The client pivoted their X/Twitter campaign to directly address the feedback with a self-aware, humorous tone, effectively neutralizing the criticism pre-launch. They gained invaluable insight into the different perceptions on each platform.
    - Added +40% in positive sentiment on X post-campaign shift.
    - Avoided a potential brand misstep at a critical moment.

  • THE CLIENT: A national real estate investment trust.

    THE CHALLENGE: A major city was proposing new zoning laws. The client needed to understand the potential impact on property values and development opportunities in specific neighborhoods, but the relevant documents were scattered across dozens of city council, planning department, and public record websites.

    THE MRI SCAN: We built a centralized data pipeline scraping all relevant municipal government websites, tracking the status of zoning proposals, public commentary, council member voting records, and related permit applications.

    THE HIDDEN TRUTH: Our analysis uncovered that proposals for upzoning (allowing taller buildings) were highly correlated with a 6-9 month prior increase in public infrastructure grant applications in those same areas—a leading indicator the client had never considered.

    THE RESULT: The client acquired properties in three key neighborhoods before the zoning changes were finalized, securing them at a lower cost and positioning themselves for a 300% increase in development potential.
    - Acquired strategic assets 6 months ahead of competitors.
    - Unlocked 300% greater development density on purchased land.

  • THE CLIENT: A regional electronics retailer competing with major online giants.

    THE CHALLENGE: They were losing sales on key products like headphones and smartwatches but didn't know if it was due to uncompetitive pricing, poor product selection, or both. Manual monitoring was impossible at scale.

    THE MRI SCAN: We implemented daily monitoring of their product catalog against identical or equivalent products on Amazon, Walmart, and Best Buy. We tracked not just price, but also availability, stock-out duration, promotional badges, and review ratings.

    THE HIDDEN TRUTH: The data revealed a dual problem. On top-selling items, their prices were 5-8% above the competition. More critically, the data showed they were consistently missing 3 of the top 5 best-selling models in the "wireless earbuds under $100" category, creating a major assortment gap.

    THE RESULT: The client dynamically adjusted their pricing strategy and, using the sales rank data as justification, added the missing high-demand SKUs to their assortment.
    - Gained +18% conversion rate on targeted electronics categories.
    - Added +12% average order value by capturing cross-sales from new, popular products.

  • THE CLIENT: A large used car dealership group specializing in high-volume acquisitions.

    THE CHALLENGE: Their buyers were relying on intuition and fragmented data to bid at online wholesale auctions (like Manheim, ACV, etc.), leading to frequent overpaying or missing undervalued vehicles.

    THE MRI SCAN: We deployed a system to collect and analyze data from major online auction platforms, tracking final sale prices, vehicle condition reports (MVRs), mileage, and time-to-sale for hundreds of thousands of transactions.

    THE HIDDEN TRUTH: Our analysis identified a consistent pricing anomaly for specific model years of a popular SUV. Vehicles with minor cosmetic flaws in the interior were being sold at a 25%+ discount, despite the repair cost being minimal. This "hidden" value signal was being missed by the market.

    THE RESULT: Armed with this precise valuation insight, the client's buyers targeted these undervalued SUVs, achieving a 38% higher profit margin on these units after low-cost reconditioning.
    - Gained +38% Profit Margin on targeted vehicle segment.
    - Achieved 15% Reduction in overall acquisition costs by avoiding overpriced auctions.

  • THE CLIENT: A national home services franchise (e.g., plumbing, HVAC).

    THE CHALLENGE: Customer churn was increasing, but the company couldn't pinpoint the root cause. Feedback was siloed across dozens of review sites, and they lacked a unified view of their brand's reputation.

    THE MRI SCAN: We implemented a comprehensive sentiment scan across Trustpilot, Yelp, Amazon Services, and Google My Business. We aggregated and analyzed thousands of reviews using NLP to identify common themes in both positive and negative feedback.

    THE HIDDEN TRUTH: The data revealed that while customers praised the quality of the work (a strength), a overwhelming 65% of negative sentiment was tied to a single, unexpected issue: a lack of clear and consistent communication regarding technician arrival times. This was a critical operational flaw masked as general dissatisfaction.

    THE RESULT: The client revamped their scheduling and customer notification system, leading to a dramatic improvement in customer experience.
    - Achieved 45% reduction in negative reviews within 3 months.
    - Gained 22-point increase in average Net Promoter Score (NPS).

  • THE CLIENT: A leading independent mortgage broker operating nationally.

    THE CHALLENGE: In a volatile interest rate environment, the client struggled to provide clients with the most competitive loan offers in real-time. Manually tracking competitors' rates across dozens of banks (like JP Morgan, Citi, Wells Fargo) was slow and error-prone.

    THE MRI SCAN: We built a system to perform daily scans of public-facing rate sheets and promotional loan terms from the top 20 national and regional banks, tracking rates for 30-year fixed, FHA, and jumbo loans.

    THE HIDDEN TRUTH: Our analysis uncovered that while major banks adjusted rates slowly, several regional banks were using an aggressive, short-term "teaser" strategy, offering rates 0.25% - 0.5% lower than the market for 48-hour windows to capture high-value applicants—a pattern invisible to manual checking.

    THE RESULT: The broker integrated our real-rate alerts into their advisor dashboard, allowing them to instantly match clients with the most advantageous lenders.
    - Added +15% in closed-loan volume by winning more client bids.
    - Improved client satisfaction by consistently securing rates in the top 10th percentile.
